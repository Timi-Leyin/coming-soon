About HirePesin

Who We Are !!

HirePesin saves you the stress of going out to look for someone who can come do a little job for you.

Hirepesin is an innovative platform that is focused on connecting users who are in need of services or quick fix jobs to top-notch and professional artisans or local craftsman around your area. We want to break the chain of unemployment and under employment amongst the youths and artisans.

Get your hustle online with HirePesin, whatever it is you do, we have created a modernized way for your services to be hired and managed without stress.

Get access to artisans and people seeking artisans using the HirePesin app, stress-free!

FAQ

FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTION

• Can I hire a person using the app without creating an account?
Absolutely not, you need to create an account to find artisans who are nearby, and also artisans need to create an account too in order for them to appear when searched for.
• How much does it cost to sign up?
It is free! But meanwhile there are minor charges for in-app transactions.
• Is there any age restriction for creating an account?
Yes, we care about the safety of anyone using the app and hence, a user has to be at least 15...
• How do I know if the artisan recommended is qualified, is there any rating?
There are ratings, once an artisan does a job for you, you can leave a rating which is on the 5 star scale, and our algorithms get to do rest as it gives artisans with higher rating much visibility when searched for.
• long does it take for an artisan to arrive?
It will take an average of 30-60 minutes for an artisan to arrive, once you have pinged or dialed up.
